Description

Vortex Wind Sensor is a rugged, proven anemometer. Supplied without any electronics, the Vortex sensor is ideal for "do-it-yourself" projects of all kinds. a high quality 3-cup rotor pressed on a stainless steel shaft, a flat aluminium mounting bracket with 2 holes. Converting pulses to wind speed is simple using the formula: 2.5 mph per Hz (1 Hz = 1 pulse/second)
